Types of Men's Suits, Styles and Details.

  • Classic Fit

    Price : Rs.4000

    For those who don't want to overthink the process, a classic fit suit is a way to go. This type of suit for men is comfortable in feel and unfettered in design, this suit style provides breathability without resorting to a loose or unkept aesthetic-they don-t call it "classic" for nothing.

  • Modern Fit

    Price : Rs.2000

    If you don't think you can pull off the slim fit suit just yet, but want to exude a heightened sartorial sensibility, by all means, consider the modern fit suit. These types of suits for men hover squarely in the realm between slim fit and a classic fit.

  • Shawl Lapel

    Price : Rs.5000

    One look at a shawl lapel and its smooth, uninterrupted lining, and you're already picturing an elegant black-tie affair. That's because this type of suit lapel is more or less exclusively found on formal wear like tuxedos and fancy dinner jackets.

  • Single Breasted

    Price : Rs.3000

    Among types of men's suits, the single-breasted suit is the most ubiquitous. The easiest way to spot one is to look for the inclusion of either one, two or three buttons along the seam, or just observe what about 99% of professional men wear to work to every day.
